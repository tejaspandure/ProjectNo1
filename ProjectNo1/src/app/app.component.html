Navigating AI's Future: Data Leaders at the Forefront


As the digital landscape evolves, generative artificial intelligence (AI) is becoming a pivotal tool for businesses worldwide. This wave of AI adoption isn't just about staying ahead – it's a necessity for competitive survival. However, integrating AI is not without its challenges. In this comprehensive report, based on a survey of 600 data leaders, you’ll uncover key trends and challenges in AI integration, including:

  • Why over 45% of data leaders have now embraced generative AI in business operations
  • Strategies to overcome data quality issues as the key hurdle in AI effectiveness
  • A plan among data leaders to enhance data management, focusing on AI readiness and governance for 2024
